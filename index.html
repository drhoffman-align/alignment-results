Quarterly Recalibration: Preventing Complacency

At 90%, your biggest risk isn't failure—it's complacency.

Every 90 Days (4x/Year)

Block 2 hours alone. No distractions. Answer these five questions:

  1. "Am I still moving toward what matters, or am I just moving well?"
    Are your actions aligned with your deepest values? Or are you just maintaining the status quo really efficiently?
  2. "What am I saying no to?"
    High performers say yes too often. What needs boundaries? What's draining energy without adding meaning?
  3. "Who am I becoming?"
    Not just what you're doing, but who you're evolving into. Do you like that person?
  4. "What would 95% alignment look like? Do I want that?"
    You're at 90%. What's the next 5%? Is it worth pursuing? Or is 90% actually your sweet spot?
  5. "Am I building something that lasts or just building my resume?"
    Legacy check. Will this matter in 10 years? What will outlive your career?

This is how you stay at 90%+ for the next 20 years.
