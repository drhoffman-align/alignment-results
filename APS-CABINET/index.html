Recommended Resources

Books

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear — This book provides a practical framework for building the small, consistent habits that create lasting energy and momentum when you're feeling scattered or running on empty.
  • The One Thing by Gary Keller — Perfect for cutting through overwhelm by teaching you to focus on the single most important action that will move you toward your goals, rather than getting paralyzed by too many options.

Practices

  • Morning Energy Anchor — Choose one non-negotiable morning habit (sleep schedule, 10-minute walk, or eating breakfast) and do it consistently for two weeks before adding anything else.
  • Weekly Goal Sprint — Every Sunday, identify one specific, measurable action you can complete by Friday that moves you toward your bigger vision, then track daily progress.

Tools

  • Copilot Money — Automatically tracks all your accounts, spending, and investments in one place, available at copilot.money and through their iPhone, iPad, and Mac apps.
  • Todoist — A task management app that helps you capture goals and break them into actionable steps with due dates and project organization, available at todoist.com.
