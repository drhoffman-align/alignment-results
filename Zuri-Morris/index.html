Recommended Resources

Books

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear — This book provides the exact framework for building consistent routines that stick, even under pressure, which directly addresses your challenge of maintaining healthy habits when life gets demanding.
  • Get Good with Money by Tiffany Aliche — Written by an African American financial educator, this book breaks down money management into simple, achievable steps that reduce overwhelm and build the financial clarity you need.
  • My Grandmother's Hands by Resmaa Menakem — This powerful book helps you understand how past experiences live in your body and provides practical tools for releasing old patterns that resurface during stress.

Practices

  • Non-Negotiable Sleep Anchor — Set one consistent bedtime and wake time that you protect regardless of other life demands, creating stability that supports all your other habits.
  • Weekly Money Check-In — Spend 15 minutes every Sunday reviewing where your money went that week and setting one financial intention for the coming week.

Tools
