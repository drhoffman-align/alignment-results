Your Personalized Recommendations

Your scores reveal someone who's navigating a period of significant transition with remarkable self-awareness in some areas while feeling uncertain in others. You demonstrate exceptional gratitude and strong goal-setting abilities, yet you're experiencing tension around understanding your natural temperament, finding clarity in your work's purpose, and building financial security. This pattern suggests you're someone who knows where you want to go and appreciates what you have, but you're still figuring out the most authentic path to get there and the practical foundation to support it.

Your lowest scoring areas — temperament awareness, work purpose, and financial intentionality — aren't weaknesses to fix but rather the precise places where focused attention will unlock the most momentum. Research in positive psychology shows that when we understand our natural temperament, we can design our lives to work with our energy rather than against it. Similarly, clarity about work purpose and financial direction creates what psychologists call "cognitive ease" — when these foundational elements align, decision-making becomes significantly less draining and more intuitive.

Given that you're working through some foundational questions about who you are and how you want to move through the world, exploring these patterns with a therapist could be valuable. Sometimes the beliefs and assumptions we formed earlier in life need updating as we grow, and a therapist helps you examine what you believe and why — creating space for patterns that truly serve you now. Once that groundwork feels more solid, working with a coach can help translate your natural goal-setting strength into consistent action across all areas of your life.
