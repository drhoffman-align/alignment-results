Recommended Resources

Books

  • Set Boundaries, Find Peace by Nedra Glover Tawwab — This book directly addresses the challenge of showing up authentically in relationships while managing the tendency to perform or edit yourself around others.
  • Get Good with Money by Tiffany Aliche — A practical approach to building financial clarity and systems that transforms money anxiety into confidence through simple, achievable steps.

Practices

  • Authenticity Check-ins — Before entering social or professional situations, pause and ask yourself "What part of me am I tempted to hide here?" then consciously choose to share one genuine thought or feeling.
  • Weekly Money Visibility — Spend 15 minutes every Sunday reviewing where your money went that week without judgment, simply building awareness of your spending patterns.

Tools

  • Copilot — A money tracking app that automatically categorizes your spending and shows clear patterns, available at copilot.money.
  • YNAB (You Need A Budget) — A budgeting system that helps you assign every dollar a purpose before you spend it, creating the financial structure that reduces money anxiety at youneedabudget.com.

Professional Support

  • Therapy for Authenticity and Money Patterns — A therapist who specializes in relationship dynamics and financial anxiety can help you understand the deeper patterns behind impression management and money avoidance, giving you tools to show up more genuinely in all areas.
